GREEN TEA MILLE CREPE CAKE

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 50g sugar
  • 280ml milk
  • 100g cake flour
  • 2T green tea powder
  • 25ml vegetable oil
  • 1T green tea powder
  • 12ml warm water
  • 185ml whipping cream
  • 50g sugar whipped cream
  • green tea powder

Steps

Entire Steps

Click me to see entire steps

1. Put eggs, sugar, and milk in a bowl and mix them.
2. Sift together the wheat flour and green tea powder and add cooking oil.
3. Mix well and sieve.
4. Heat the coating pan over low heat.
5. Pour batter a little bit over low heat and coat it with thin
6. It's time to flip it when you inflate it like it's bubbling on the top.
7. It's got to be at least 15.
8. Cool the baked sheet.
9. When the sheet cools, mix green tea and warm water well.
10. Put whipped cream, sugar, and mixed green tea water in a large bowl and whip it hard.
11. Place a sheet on a bowl and apply cream thinly.
12. Put another sheet on top of it and apply cream.
13. Repeat 15-20 sheets and do not apply cream to the top sheet.
14. Set the cream in the fridge for at least 1 hour.
15. Wipe out the whipped cream and decorate the cake. Decorize it with green tea, sprinkles. Eat the cake in three days.
