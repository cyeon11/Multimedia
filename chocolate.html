STRAWBERRY PAVE CHOCOLATE

Ingredients

  • 400g strawberry chocolate
  • 120ml fresh cream (whipping cream)
  • 15ml starch syrup
  • 20g strawberry powder

Steps

Entire Steps

Click me to see entire steps

1. Dissolve chocolate in a double boiler or microwave oven (don't melt too high or water in it).
2. Put whipped cream and starch syrup in a pot and off fire when the edges boil.
3. Mix melted chocolate with boiled ingredients. (Mix lightly. If stirred quickly, air bubbles will appear.)
4. Pour the well-mixed chocolate into the prepared mold.
5. Place in airtight container or cover with plastic wrap and let stand in refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
6. Remove from the mold and cut the chocolates into easy-to-eat sizes (dip the knife in hot water, wipe off the water, then cut neatly).
7. Put the strawberry powder on the outside.(powder or mashed dry strawberries)
8. Cover the chocolate and refrigerate it. Finish it within a week.
