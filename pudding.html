CHOCOLATE BANANA PUDDING

Ingredients

  • 35g unsalted butter
  • 20g sugar
  • 35g egg white
  • 2ml vanilla extract
  • 70g cake flour
  • 10g cocoa powder
  • 25ml milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 20g sugar
  • 4ml vanilla extract
  • 30g cornstarch
  • 250g milk
  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 300g whipping cream
  • two bananas

Steps

Entire Steps

Click me to see entire steps

1. Put butter in a bowl and loosen it.
2. Add sugar, whites, and vanilla extract and mix.
3. When mixed evenly, sift together the flour and cocoa powder. Add milk and mix.
4. If you can't see the raw powder, put it in squeeze bag.
5. Squeeze on top of pan in appropriate sizes.
6. Bake in a preheated 170 degree oven for 10 to 12 minutes and let cool.
7. Make cream while it cools.
8. Put milk and vanilla in a pot and heat them on low heat to heat the steam.
9. Put the eggs and sugar in the bowl and mix.
10. Add corn starch and mix.
11. Add heated milk and mix.
12. Put the ingredients back in the pot and put them on medium heat.
13. Create custard cream by stirring constantly so that the bottom is not cooked.
14. When it starts to stick together, it'll stir faster.
15. When the concentration is as shown in the picture, lower the heat and mix the dark chocolate evenly.
16. Pour cream into a flat bowl and pat the wrap to cool in the fridge.
17. When the cream cools, put the whipped cream in a clean bowl and slice the banana.
18. Put custard cream on a sieve.
19. Use whipped cream whippers to loosen the custard cream once more.
20. Mix whipped cream and custard cream evenly.
21. Add bananas and cookies and mix them roughly.
22. Put it in a cup or bowl and make it cold in the fridge. + Eat all your desserts within a day or two!
