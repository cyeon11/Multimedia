VANILLA CANELES

Ingredients

  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 500ml milk
  • 40g butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 125g cake flour
  • 1 egg
  • 45g egg yolk
  • 20ml rum
  • melted beeswax or butter

Steps

Entire Steps

Click me to see entire steps

1. Scrape the vanilla bean in half and put both the skin and seeds in a pot.
2. Put milk and butter together in a pot and boil over low heat.
3. In the other bowl, mix the sugar and the flour.
4. Pour boiling ingredients into a bowl and stir gently.
5. Mix the egg with the rum and pour it into the slightly cooled ingredients.
6. 12 to 24 hours in the chilled dough to seal the mixed.
7. Put the dough down on a sieve and melt the wax in a double boiler.
8. Coat to the mold with melted wax or placed at room temperature butter.
9. Bake prepared dough in preheated oven by filling 90% of the mold.
10. Turn over and remove from the frame + seal and refrigerate (eat within 3-4 days).
